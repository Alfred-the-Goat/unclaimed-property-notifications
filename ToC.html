2. Program Description

The Unclaimed Property Notification Service contacts organizations to inform them that they may have unclaimed property (such as uncashed checks, deposits, or other financial assets) held by a state government. Our goal is to help organizations become aware of and reclaim funds that rightfully belong to them.

We identify potential matches between state unclaimed property databases and publicly listed organizations, then reach out via SMS, email, or phone to provide notification and guidance on the claims process.

This is a free informational service. We do not charge recipients for notifications, and we are not affiliated with any state government agency.
