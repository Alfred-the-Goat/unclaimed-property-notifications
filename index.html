Unclaimed Property Notification Service

Operated by Levi Dugan

What We Do

We help organizations discover and reclaim unclaimed property held by state governments. Unclaimed property can include uncashed checks, forgotten deposits, insurance proceeds, and other financial assets that have been turned over to the state after a period of inactivity.

Many organizations are unaware that they have funds waiting to be claimed. We identify potential matches between state unclaimed property databases and publicly listed organizations, then reach out to let them know.

This is a free informational service. We do not charge for notifications, and we are not affiliated with any state government agency.

How It Works

We cross-reference publicly available state unclaimed property records with publicly listed organizations. When we find a potential match, we send a one-time notification via SMS, email, or phone to let the organization know that unclaimed funds may be available to them. We then provide guidance on how to file a claim through the appropriate state program.
