Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Levi Dugan ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our Unclaimed Property Notification Service ("Service"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of all individuals and organizations we contact.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and use the following categories of information solely for the purpose of delivering unclaimed property notifications:

  • Organization name — as listed in public state unclaimed property databases.
  • Mailing address — as listed in public state unclaimed property databases and public business directories.
  • Phone number — as publicly listed by the organization on platforms such as Google Maps, organizational websites, and public business directories.
  • Email address — as publicly listed on organizational websites.

We do not collect personal information about individual people. All data pertains to organizations and is sourced exclusively from publicly available records.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used strictly for the following purpose:

To notify organizations that they may have unclaimed property held by a state government, and to provide guidance on how to claim it.

We may contact organizations via SMS, email, or phone call to deliver this notification. SMS messages are one-time, informational notifications — not recurring marketing communications.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, lease, trade, or otherwise share your information with any third parties. We do not share information for marketing purposes. Your data is used exclusively for delivering unclaimed property notifications as described in this policy.

4. Data Retention

We retain contact information only for as long as necessary to complete the notification process. Once a notification has been delivered and any follow-up has concluded, contact information is deleted from our active systems within 90 days.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we handle from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Access to contact data is limited to authorized personnel only.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request that we delete any information we hold about your organization.
  • Opt out of any future communications by replying STOP to any SMS message, or by contacting us directly.
  • Request information about what data we hold regarding your organization.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
